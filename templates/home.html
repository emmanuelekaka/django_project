What exactly is KYUMA-KYUMSA

KYUMSA(Kyambogo University Muslim Students Association) is an association in Kyambogo University that aims at creating a condusive environment for all muslim students through a series of activities that create unity and confidence in them. Promote brotherhood and support in every angle so as to promote and improove onselves during and after campus

Learn More

Mission

To serve Muslim students and facilitate thier efforts to establish, develop, and maintain their spiritual and academic standards

Vission

Creating a safe space in which Muslim students build a closer relationship with Allah and their community

Core values

Concern for others, Honesty, Integrity & Reliability

About us

Background

The association was formed in 1977 with the aim of uniting Muslim students in the three sister institutions: ITEK (Institute Teacher Education Kyambogo), UPK(Uganda Polytechnic Kyambogo), UNISE (Uganda National Institute Of Special Needs )
It was first named kYAMSA (Kyambogo Muslim Students Association) since kyambogo had not yet become a university rather they were just institutions that were neighboring each other.
